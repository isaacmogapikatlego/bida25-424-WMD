About Urban Tails

Gaborone is changing. More young professionals have more disposable incomes, more apartments and more people treating their pets like actual children. But there is a gap in market in this industry of pet care.

Options in Botswana are either informal neighbourhood arrangements with little accountability or traditional kennels that feel institutional and impersonal. Nobody is doing luxury or going above and beyond to make sure that our pets are treated like family. Urban Tails was founded to own that space.

Premium Pet Sitting

Real care overnight stays where sitter sleeps on your couch, so your anxious dog is never alone.

Luxury Product Boutique

Well-curated high-end products that are highly unavailable in Gaborone — products that belong in a design magazine.

The Doorman Service

For an annual fee of P250, Urban Tails securely stores your house keys when you are out of town.
